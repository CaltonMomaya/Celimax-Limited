About Celimax Motors

Celimax Motors Ltd is Mombasa's premier automotive dealership, dedicated to providing quality vehicles and exceptional customer service. With years of experience in the automotive industry, we have built a reputation for reliability, transparency, and value.

The Visionary Behind Celimax: Isaac Irungu

Isaac Irungu's journey in the automotive industry began over 6 years ago as a young apprentice in a small garage in Nairobi. His passion for cars and innate business acumen quickly set him apart, and by age 25, he had established his first car dealership.

Driven by a vision to make quality vehicles accessible to all Kenyans, Isaac founded Celimax Motors in 2015. His philosophy centers on transparency, customer satisfaction, and building lasting relationships rather than just making sales.

"Every car we sell represents a dream fulfilled, a business empowered, or a family connected," says Isaac. "That's why we treat each customer with the respect and attention they deserve."

Under Isaac's leadership, Celimax Motors has grown from a small lot with five vehicles to Mombasa's largest automotive marketplace, serving thousands of satisfied customers across Kenya and beyond.

Frequently Asked Questions

What payment options do you accept? +
We accept various payment methods including cash, bank transfers, mobile money, and financing options through our partner financial institutions. We also offer flexible payment plans for qualified buyers.
Do you offer warranties on your vehicles? +
Yes, we offer warranties on all our vehicles. The duration and coverage depend on the vehicle's age, condition, and whether it's a fresh import or locally used. Our team will provide detailed warranty information for each vehicle.
Can I test drive a vehicle before purchasing? +
Absolutely! We encourage all potential buyers to test drive vehicles they're interested in. Simply visit our showroom in Mombasa, and our team will arrange a test drive at your convenience.
How does the direct import process work? +
Our direct import service allows you to purchase vehicles from international markets. We handle all aspects including sourcing, shipping, customs clearance, and registration. The process typically takes 4-8 weeks depending on the source country.
Do you provide vehicle history reports? +
Yes, we provide comprehensive vehicle history reports for all our cars. These include information about previous ownership, accident history, service records, and more to ensure transparency in your purchase.

What is your return policy? +
We offer a 7-day return policy on all vehicles, subject to certain conditions. The vehicle must be returned in the same condition as purchased, with no additional mileage or damage. Please contact our team for detailed information about our return policy.
Do you offer delivery services? +
Yes, we offer delivery services across Kenya. Delivery costs vary depending on the location. We can also arrange for international shipping for customers outside Kenya.
Can I trade in my current vehicle? +
Yes, we accept trade-ins. Our team will evaluate your current vehicle and provide a fair market value that can be applied towards your new purchase from Celimax Motors.
