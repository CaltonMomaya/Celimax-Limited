About Celimax Motors

Isaac Iringu Gitigi

Founder & CEO of Celimax Motors Ltd

Isaac's journey in the automotive industry began humbly as a salesman at a small car dealership in Mombasa. With an unwavering passion for vehicles and an innate understanding of customer needs, he quickly rose through the ranks, becoming the top-performing salesman within his first year.

In 2020, with a vision to revolutionize car buying in Mombasa, Isaac took a leap of faith and founded Celimax Motors Ltd. Starting with just three pre-owned vehicles and a small office space, his determination and customer-centric approach quickly set Celimax apart in the competitive automotive market.

"I remember those early days vividly - working late into the night, personally ensuring each vehicle was in perfect condition, and building relationships with every customer who walked through our doors. My experience as a salesman taught me that trust is the most valuable currency in this business."

Under Isaac's leadership, Celimax Motors has grown exponentially, becoming Mombasa's largest automotive marketplace. The company now boasts an extensive inventory of both new and pre-owned vehicles, offering flexible payment options including cash, hire purchase, and financing arrangements tailored to meet diverse customer needs.

Isaac's philosophy is simple: "Every customer deserves to drive their dream car, regardless of their budget." This commitment to accessibility and quality has earned Celimax Motors a reputation as the most trusted vehicle dealership in the region.

Today, Celimax Motors continues to expand, with plans to open additional branches across Kenya while maintaining the personal touch and commitment to excellence that Isaac established from day one.
